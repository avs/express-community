Cube on Surface

Description

This is the standard AVS/Express interp_data module. Respect to the original one has the following modifications:

  • Use the STM3 cache dimension settings.
  • More convenient input ports.

Gives to a surface the values of the volume in which the surface is immersed in the positions occupied by the surface itself.

Input Ports

surface Field The surface that must receive the values.
cube Field The volume field with the values.

Parameters

None

Output Ports

out DefaultObject The renderable surface.
